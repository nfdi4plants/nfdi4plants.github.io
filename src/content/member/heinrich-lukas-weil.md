Hey, I'm Lukas! I am a core developer of DataPLANT, where I focus especially on maintaining and driving forward specification changes according to community needs - including [ARC Scaffold](https://github.com/nfdi4plants/ARC-specification) and [ARC RO-Crate](https://github.com/nfdi4plants/arc-ro-crate-profile). To aid in community adoption, I maintain [ARCtrl](https://github.com/nfdi4plants/ARCtrl), a polyglot library available in the [.NET](https://www.nuget.org/packages/ARCtrl/), [Python](https://pypi.org/project/arctrl/), and [JavaScript](https://www.npmjs.com/package/@nfdi4plants/arctrl) ecosystems.

Besides this technical work, I particularly enjoy the social aspects of the RDM-world, such as engaging with the community on hackathons, organizing such events ([1](https://nfdi4plants.github.io/events/2024-04-08_boppard-arc-symposium/),[2](https://nfdi4plants.github.io/events/2025-06-23_kaiserslautern_arc-hackathon/),[3](https://nfdi4plants.github.io/events/2026-05-25_kaiserslautern_arc-hackathon/)), and helping to onboard new users and developers.
