DataPLANT presented in a NFDI special edition of Bausteine FDM

28 Jul 2021

We are pleased to draw your attention to the new issue of the Open Access journal "Bausteine Forschungsdatenmanagement". In this special issue, various consortia of the first round in the National Research Data Infrastructure (NFDI) are presented. In cooperation with the Board of Directors of the NFDI, a booklet has been created in which consortia from the various disciplines introduce themselves and describe the tasks and challenges in the field of research data management. All contributions are available online, the contribution on DataPLANT is linked here.

The online journal "Bausteine Forschungsdatenmanagement" is aimed at practitioners who want to share concrete experiences with the management of research data and recommendations for action derived from them. The publication is a result of the DINI/nestor WG "Research Data", which aims, among other things, to improve the networking of the German research data landscape. "Bausteine Forschungsdatenmanagement" therefore aims to promote exchange and discussion on the topic.
